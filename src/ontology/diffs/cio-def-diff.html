Terms with changed definitions

CIO:0000012 : confidence statement from congruent evidence lines of multiple types, overall confidence high

(was confidence statement from congruent evidence lines of multiple types, best confidence high)

OLD: A confidence statement from congruent evidence lines of multiple types, the best supporting evidence being a highly reliable evidence.
NEW: A confidence statement from congruent evidence lines of multiple types, of an overall high confidence level.

CIO:0000013 : confidence statement from congruent evidence lines of multiple types, overall confidence medium

(was confidence statement from congruent evidence lines of multiple types, best confidence medium)

OLD: A confidence statement from congruent evidence lines of multiple types, the best supporting evidence being a moderately trusted evidence.
NEW: A confidence statement from congruent evidence lines of multiple types, of an overall moderately trusted confidence level.
